1. Introduction

Welcome to the Atlanta Socializers Club website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Sign up for our newsletter
  • RSVP for events
  • Complete forms on our website
  • Contact us directly

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Preferences and interests

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Pages you view
  • Time spent on pages
  • Links you click
  • Other browsing information

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Process event RSVPs and registrations
  • Send you email newsletters and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer service
  • Personalize your experience on our website
  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Protect against unauthorized access and legal liability

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger or acquisition

5. Your Choices

You can control your information in the following ways:

  • Update or correct your personal information by contacting us
  • Opt-out of receiving our newsletters or promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions
  • Adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@atlantasocializers.org
